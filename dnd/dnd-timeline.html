• Dark Era

  • Forming the Astral Sea
    Mceeju became, and the Astral Sea was formed. After this Uke, Veri and Xeculus were made from Mceeju.
  • Xeculus' Shattering
    Xeculus was seperated into 3, forming Lcaoc, Nmei and Tharizdun.
  • Vihi's Arrival
    Vihi arrived, and with it, Uke and Veri were united to be never seperated again.
  • Forging of Life
    With the requests of Lcaoc, Veri bore Cyaxo and Ehx. With the sight of Nmei, Veri bore Pwazo, Buib and Cfofb. After Tharizdun's echoes, Vesvjdegz born.
  • Addiction of Giving
    After Veri loved what it experienced, and loved, it bore Gud and Wodroh. Naahv also formed after Lcaoc's request from Uke. After not stopping, gods started to dispose of their power in forms of other divine beings, and the divine was formed fully.
  • The 5th Generation
    Lwaqy was formed from Naahv,
    Lwaro and Radu was formed from Gud,
    Modgo was formed from Wodroh.
  • The 6th Generation
    6th generation included deities like Raxcoh, that represent an act instead of a fact. They were introduced as the first steps towards deities split to their respective domains.
  • Pedi's Creation
    Out of Lwaqy's blood, pouring from his burned eyes, Pedi would born. Lwaqy being impressed by these creatures sent them to his home plane.
  • Introduction of Domains
    Nature, Tempest, Trickery, Death, Knowledge, War, Life and, Light domains were formed. With this, deities split and this split would eventualy call for the downfall of cosmos.
  • Next Generation
    Domain deities have grouped up to create more and more holy beings.

  • Divine Era

  • Forming of the Outs
    Outer Planes and the Outlands passageway was formed with the collaboration of the Divine. They were both barren, filled with Astral Sea and portals to nothingness.
  • Forming of the Worlds
    Inner Planes marble was formed, with it Overworld, Underworld, and the Elemental Chaos. They were also populated with flora and fauna. After animals refused to worship, they were banished to the forest.
  • Appearance of Overseers
    Overseer planes such as Arcadia and Carseri fill the void that is Astral Sea. Demiplanes also take place outside the Inner Planes. To populate them, Gods formed man after a feud of power within. Their creations dissolved because they lacked.
  • Appearance of the Primordials
    The elements without soul that forgot their makers. They were made to create and populate aswell, so they shared some sort of Divinity. This allowed them to rule their respective places within the Elemental Chaos.
  • The 8th Generation
    Cfofb, Gond, Umberlee, Kurtulmak, Mielikki, Tezr, Nizi, Lunm, Cyani, Nepi, Ayrugcumoak, Afez, Wusor, Wugu, Gaajyx arrived to their respective domains.
  • The 9th Generation
    Gezi, Pior, Czeeg, Waqorafe arrived to their respective domains.
  • The Final Generation
    Megm, Wadqwofm, Spina, Xua, Fexyiaf, Moradin, Corellon, Gruumsh arrived to their respective domains.
  • Forming of the Inner Planes
    Inside the Elemental Chaos, affected with Overworld and Underworld, the Inner Planes were formed in a balanced state. These included Nhaft, Teothe, Earth and many others.

  • Johke Era

  • The First Generation
    With the joint work of the Divine, First generation to ever inhabit the Inner Planes were born. Pedgj was the first.
  • Battles of Abyss
    Creatures from the Abyss, banished ones and the lost ones formed armies to attack Overseer planes.
  • Craftsmen of Nhaft
    Lwaro was finally chained thanks to the chain which did not exist. This in turn had some relieving effect on Uke and Veri, the lifegivers.
  • 4 Cardinals, 4 Colors
    To help guide the Inner Planes, Lcohwuj was located. References to the Lcohwuj are found in the writings. At some point, the brothers became associated with the figure of Ecqig, an Johke sage whom took care of the Lcohwuj.
  • Wiob ascends
    Wiob, after all his achievements are granted Divinity due to many Rixm Liox followers depicting him as Divine. The root cause for this was that both Susaih and Wiob emerged back from their travels to the Underworld. This is the one and only mortal ascension.
  • The Nahuehak
    Msauh, to avoid death and gain an equal footing with the Great Sage Ecqig, struck a deal with Pedi. Msauh then made a Nhaft smith forge the soul devourer blade, Nahuehak. Naahv, delighted with this gift took Msauh near him and made Fexyiaf test the blade on the strong Lcohwuj, Uufh. This act scarred Msauh since they were once like brothers but deep inside, he wanted to be equal with them. After Uufh's death, a creator god placed Msauh to his position since he knew all the things required and he'd suffer in his once-brother's spot.
  • Black Rain
    Lwaro kills Raxy, Mceeju wakes up and sees that both the individual tries of the Divine and the feud amongst them is out of hand. He casts Black Rain all around the cosmos, returning the marble to a clean slate. Black Rain shakes the Outer Marble, making Johke lore travel between many planes.

  • Ancient Era

  • Aboleth Invasion
    Unfortunately with the weak state of the Cosmos, creatures from the marble's outer planes and further invaded the inner planes, all the way to the Outlands.
  • Wiob's Virtue
    Once Divine, after realizing that Gugig will never come to his side, Wiob falls for Vihi, the goddess of love and beauty. After wooing her with mortal ways, Vihi experiences something uncommon, and they bring Gaxaca to the world. This makes lifegivers angry and to save Gaxaca, Vihi banishes him to Inner Planes, forming the very first Aasimar lineage.
  • Allowance of Nizi
    Radu and Nizi marry, with Uke's divine to grant Nizi immortality and Mceeju's approval.
  • Ancient Peace
    After learning their lesson, the Divine joined forces to create an ultimate race. Based off of Mceeju itself, mimicking him with the understanding of all the Divine's essence. Finally, the cosmos was at peace.
  • Ancient Knowledge
    After some myriad, the race finally understood the reason for their existence, and Mceeju itself. They first used this understanding to create artifacts as a civilization, signifying that they have reached divinity as a group.
  • Ethereal Darkness
    Trying to create their own Divine, they abused every balance and this ended up killing Wusor, the Weave. Onposufm preventing this consumed all that is Ancient and formed Wusor with a dragon form. With their final moments, Ancients left a warning that is too difficult to grasp.

  • The Magical Stick 494 BC
    Wodroh becomes stuck in a vase although he was released really hastily by his followers. He left the farmers alone for achieving this feat, mostly because he believed the tale of magical stick was true.
  • Cutting of N' Tiora 356 BC
    Because of greed, the tip of Altioh Cape was exploded to stop the growth of the giant tree. All it's roots are also cleared. Altioh the town was renamed to Atioh, because many believed that the name no longer stood intact.
  • Forming of Slayer's Take 287 BC
    After the failure that is Mujina Army, the Serpents who cleared them out got shunned and rebranded to Slayer's Take.
  • Reckoning 230 BC
    "The action of estimating" was the words struck while talking about this event, but it drew many elves insane overnight, due to a being invading their trance and showing them its vision of future. This is believed to be the main reason why Schzamath is run by male drow instead of female.
  • First Artifact 131 BC
    The first man-made artifact was crafted. This was supposed to be impossible but with enough bargain from Warlock patrons and Divine sacrifices -such as souls- this feat was achieved. Then, in an instant, the ones involved were cursed and most banished. The artifact still stays, punished to walk the material planes for all the years remained in those harvested souls. It changes every midday and its memories vanish. Cleansing this item will grant the one cleric a throne in Life domain, and using it will help regain some amount of weave (spell-slots).
  • Worldtree 56 BC
    Crying for the world and no longer feeding as well, the worldtree, N'Tiora, lost some of its interplanar passageways, unfortunately removing the ones from existence in demiplanes, or leaving them there without a way home.
  • The Assassination of Narra 1 BC
    This event is the triggering point, the cause for the Mage's Crisis. For the way of a painless death, the Mage brought more and more pain to the worlds.

  • After Mage's Crisis

  • March 1 AC
    Commoners, non-magic users and some mages joined to march capitals of governments. Many died, many got lost and a global war against spellcasters began.
  • General Jet's Reform 3 AC
    After besting many magic-based organizations at battle, General Jet, representatives of races and the Arcanum Alliance have come to terms. The crisis was halted.
  • Wowen Route Reinitialized 6 AC
    The most important trade line that is respected by all nations and is heavily protected once again started to run. Carrying Wine and other luxury ingredients to Elven lands and materials like cosmetics, incense and citrus are moved towards Elkia. This route was heavily pillaged during war and seized operations.
  • 11 Pillars 11 AC
    Believed to be Teothe's oldest Temple, made by Iyiyohkens and renovated by the early Ancients was found in Feianthe. Puzzle of the 11 pillars -with all having symbols and connections to some magic- was never solved. Later, a paladin named Aerin Ravanora, of the Ravanora royalty stated that he saw this in a divine trip and the salvation required ancient artifacts which when used with a combination could act as a portal to different planes of existence.
  • Wrath of the Maggot Eater 26 AC
    By the defeat of a Tarrasque, Maggot Eater is believed to stood and waited for it to rot. The stench and disease caused nearby cities to evacuate. The armies fell to plague before they could even reach the Ancient One. There is no concrete proof of this event but the disease. Some believe that this was a hoax on how the plague started by the fanatics.
  • Keok's Death 27 AC
    The High King Keok died of a disease and refused the resurrection attempts. He united Elkia to be what it is after the Mage's Crisis, and lies in the Elkian Castle, the once-aboleth structure.
  • The Chamber of Secrets 33 AC
    Cassidy opens the Chamber of Secrets, a spell cast while in the dream-state that allows users to tap into the otherworldly weave. This chamber of secrets being tapped via an artifact is located in Choll, the Homeplane for the Pearl Plunderers. The effects of extensive usage can be seen in the Chronicles published by Sera Krautek, over the years until her disappearance later in The Akash Catastrophe.
  • Ignisiblings & Alean's Grave
    Somewhere along the Dwarven Union in Elkia Continent, Netheron Ignisie and Evelynn Ignisie find an Aasimar named Zeandil that becomes publicly known after 40 years of divine silence. Information on him while limited, is believed to come from his great exit of Alean's Grave with his friend Gotrek. People involved with this endeavour include Isaac Morgenstern, Sera Krautek, and Cursed Louen.
  • Peace for Akairo 47 AC
    As Red Tower became inactive by the ongoing siege, truce between the armies of Dwarven Union and Akairo was achieved by the leader of the Paladin's Guild, Corellon Tieri. An adventuring party from Feianthe named Plague of Misery oversees the situation of truce, since both nations were at neutral terms with them.
  • The Akash Catastrophe 48 AC
    Upon the sudden appearance of an Ancient Blue Dragon within The Athenaeum in Akash, a Solar -to protect the souls from a Lich's inactive phylactery- and a Lich emerged. Battling three ways, they killed over 3.5 million dwarves, many traders from the Dwarven Trade Route, important scholar Christopher A27, and Elanil Gredove. The multilayered city collapsed on its underground part called Below-Akash (or Gesag-Akash in Dwarvish) as the dragon emerged victorious. Unfortunately being grabbed by the iron golem guarding Athenaeum, the dragon hastily decided to get rid of the hostile environment and cost infinite amounts of gold in damage to the city. Tales tell that this event was triggered by a cowardly attack on the dragon, struck by William Lestrange.
